    About this blog

    This blog contains short write-ups (although I do occassionally get carried away) on probability puzzles and decision making problems. The focus is on clear reasoning, simple models, and principled solutions. The problems featured are the ones I personally found interesting which have some kind of hidden insight that is not obvious on the first glance. The solutions often reveal a deeper structure behind the problem and build upon it connecting them broader ideas in probability, statistics or decision making.

    Along the way, I try to include some related concepts and explanations that emerged while exploring the problem which provide an insight into the broader area. Even if you have seen a particular problem previously, you may still discover a different perspective or a useful insight in the discussion here. So do give it a try.

    Problems are tagged by topic and approximate difficulty to help readers navigate the collection. If you have an interesting puzzle you'd like to see explored, feel free to suggest it below. I'll try to write about it in a future post.
