# About

Hi, I'm **Palashdeep Singh**.

I work as a Quantitative Research Associate in the Equities Derivatives Group at J.P. Morgan, where I focus on model validation, derivatives risk frameworks, and quantitative infrastructure for complex products.

My academic background is in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Machine Learning from IIT Kanpur, where I developed an interest in probability and decision-making under uncertainty.

This blog is a collection of problems that I found interesting while solving these myself. Many of these puzzles reveal deeper ideas which are not apparent at once.

The goal of the blog is simple: to explore elegant problems and explain the ideas behind them with **clear reasoning and minimal machinery**.

Even simple puzzles often contain surprisingly deep structure.
